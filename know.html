18 Things to Know Before You Go to High School


1. Before you go to sleep, check that you put all your materials in your backpack.

2. Make sure you have all the things you need to do your homework before you start.

3. Always do your homework on time.

4. Put your alarm 5-15 min before you actually have to wake up.

5. Develop a relationship with your teachers.

6. It’s okay to lose friends!

7. Get involved in a club or sport.

8. Trying to be constantly cool comes off as uncool; don't try to be something you're not!

9. Be socially involved and try to make new friends.

10. Don’t be desperate to fit in. BE ORIGINAL. BE YOURSELF.

11. Stay organized!! Get a planner.

12. Speak up for yourself.

13. Attend school dances and other school activities.

14. Don’t park in the senior lot unless you’re a senior!

15. Stay away from drama as much as you can.

16. Pack an emergency bag in your locker.

17. It’s okay to pull all-nighters sometimes, but not every night.

18. Try not to take everything to heart; pick your battles!
